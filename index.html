Used Item Donations

You can donate used items either in person at one of our donation centers or by mail using USPS to our PO box. You can also use our label maker to order a box and pick up from your local USPS location.

Before You Donate!!!

Check if the games, controllers and consoles all work

Test your games before sending. Make sure your controllers don't have broken or loose parts. Plug in and turn on consoles and make sure that games are playing

Clean everything, everything is going to a Hospital after all

Wipe down any controllers or consoles and check disks for scratches or smudges. No one wants to play with a dirty controller.

Make sure all necessary wires are included in your donation

Be sure to include charging cables with controllers and power cables for consoles and any other peripherals.

How To Donate Used Items

Find a Donation Center

Enter your zip code below and find a donation center nearest to you. Partners include Goodwill, BestBuy, and Gamestop.

Send it by Mail

You can send your donation by mail by either visiting your local USPS location and mailing it to our PO Box or by using our online Shipping Wizard to order packaging and schedule a pick up.
